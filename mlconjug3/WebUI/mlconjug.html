Help

Enter a verb in the input field, select a language and subject, and press the submit button to conjugate the verb. You can also filter the conjugations by person, number, and mood using the filters on the right side of the screen. Use the verb history section to view previously conjugated verbs. The sample verbs and example sentences sections provide examples of commonly used verbs and example sentences using those verbs.
